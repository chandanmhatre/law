About Us

M/s. D. Y. Wani & Co., is a Partnership Law Firm of Mr. D. Y. Wani and Mr. Devashish Wanistarted out in April 2018. The Firm specializes in the Civil Law branches of Banking, Corporate, and Real Estate. The Law Firm represents two extensive legal professionals having diverse experience.

Mr. D. Y. Wani holds a rich and wide experience in Banking Law for over 17 years working as the Legal Head with the Bank of Maharashtra prior to voluntarily retiring in December 2014, subsequently which he started out practicing as an Advocate. Mr. Devashish Wani worked out several aspects of Corporate and Real Estate branches and has also completed his Articleship tenure of 3 yearsfor his Solicitor.

The Firm provides day-in and day-out legal assistance to their clients being Public Sector Undertakings and NBFCsfortheirlegal compliances.
